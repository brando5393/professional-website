About Me

I am a full stack web developer trained in the MERN development stack. I studied at the University of Wisconsin Coding Boot Camp, and have a passion for learning while using my skills to help others. My overall goal, is always to produce work that both meets and exceeds what a client is looking for. When I am not coding, or in the process of delving into learning about a new and interesting topic, I can be found sitting at my piano, reading a book, or by his royal decree, (I just pay the rent) showering attention and the more than occasional belly rub on my cat Peanut. If you like what you see, go ahead and contact me. I look forward to hearing from you and working together to create something amazing.
